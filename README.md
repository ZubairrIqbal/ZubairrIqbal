# 💫 About Me:
I am a Software Engineer with over two years of hands-on experience in building and deploying machine learning solutions across computer vision, large language models, and smart agriculture systems. My work spans the full ML lifecycle—from data collection and preprocessing to model training, evaluation, and production deployment—using modern frameworks such as PyTorch, Hugging Face, FastAPI, Docker, and MLflow.<br><br>I have strong experience in computer vision, working with Vision Transformers, YOLO, and CNN-based models on real-world agricultural datasets, achieving over 92% classification accuracy under noisy field conditions. Alongside this, I have designed and implemented retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) systems and fine-tuned LLaMA-2 models using LoRA and QLoRA to build efficient, domain-specific NLP solutions with reduced computational cost.<br><br>Currently, I work as an AI Research Assistant, collaborating on AI–industry projects and contributing to applied research and publications. With a background in electrical engineering and applied AI, I bring a problem-solving mindset, strong research skills, and a focus on building practical, scalable solutions
